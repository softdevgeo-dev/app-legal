Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language I'm learning?

To change your learning language, go to your Profile tab, tap on 'Language Settings', and select your desired language from our list of 68 options.

Can I download videos for offline viewing?

Yes! With our Premium plan, you can download up to 50 videos for offline viewing. Look for the download icon next to videos.

How does the video learning method work?

Our method uses authentic videos with interactive subtitles, vocabulary highlights, and comprehension quizzes to immerse you in the language naturally.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for our Premium plan. You can access all features during this period without any charge.
